Further information:
This is an assembly of working prototype & Documentation for a sample project of an interactive Zoo. The main requirements was to enable remote Feeding of animals from a web application and providing verification animals were feed, and also ensuring nutrition requirements are meet for each animal. By providing a gamification interface and a reward mechanism we can automate tasks and off load mundane tasks from zoo keepers to patreons of the facilities that wish to participate, enabling the Zoo to focus on the expansion effort for 2019!
