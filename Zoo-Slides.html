Requirements:

The end user requested an application to automate mundane tasks around the Zoo Like Feeding animals, enabling their staff to focus on zoo duties and the growth their organization needs to meet demands. The databaes already exist but some modifications will need to be made to enable these new features.

Business Justification:

With advertisements, in-app purchases, and a subscription model this application can provide a future revenue stream to help the Zoo contribute more into their expansion efforts. The customer is looking for a minimum viable product to help show this concept to the Board of Directors and onboard initial stakeholders.


Phase #2 - will build out the revenue portion and work on building our user base.


Phase #3 - Roll out missing features, requests and deploy dependencies for operations at scale.
